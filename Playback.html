Playback Singing

Playback singing is the practice of recording songs separately from the on-screen performance and then syncing the pre-recorded vocals with the visuals in movies, TV shows, or music videos.

Notable playback singers include Lata Mangeshkar, Kishore Kumar, Asha Bhosle, Arijit Singh, Shreya Ghoshal, and many others, depending on the region and language.

Playback singers need a strong and versatile voice, the ability to emote through singing, pitch accuracy, and the capacity to adapt to various musical styles.

Start by developing your singing skills, gaining experience in live performances, building a portfolio, and networking with composers, music directors, and recording studios.
